What Is CARE?

C.A.R.E. stands for Complete Automotive Repair Education/Empowerment. This seminar is designed for the basic automobile novice. The soul purpose for this seminar is to share information that is long sought after in the automotive repair consumer field.

Have you ever been in a situation where you were pressed for time? Have you ever been in a situation when you need to be somewhere in a rush, and the only means to get there is your vehicle but the time you need it the most...something fails and doesn't start! It stalls while driving or maybe you had to apply emergency brakes to save you and your loved one, or the car just isn't responding the way you need it to. These are scenarios that can happen if there is poor maintenance done on your primary source of getting from point A to point B, and that's when you realize that your car is asking you to CARE.

Well I am here to teach you how to have care for your car, and so much more!

In this engaging and interactive three hour seminar, you are going to learn automotive terms, basic maintenance and repairs, and overall guidance of your car through the skills and insight of the automotive service industry. This is an in-depth beginner's track on what to know, and what to do. The next time your car has troubles, you may know exactly just what to do.

So, if you would like an interactive approach to learning your vehicle, please R.S.V.P. and lets get Empowered!
